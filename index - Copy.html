Caseys Custom History

Caseys Custom was started out of my home garage during the summer of 2020 due to Covid. I always enjoyed woodworking and enjoyed my high school woodworking classes. I wanted to get back into it and what better time to start when all we could do was stay home during this time.

Sparked Imagination

I started out by looking up different easy crafts that I could build that weren't challenging. My very first build was a Tailgate Bench. I really enjoy making these benches and have found easier, better, and more affordable ways of building them since I've started. After getting the hang of all of my tools and creating different projects is when I had my first personal custom order of a Dinning Storage Table. This is when I realized that I could really make anything I put my mind to even if it was challenging.
